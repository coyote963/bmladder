Boring Man Ladder

Boring Man Leaderboards Track your performance.

With live ratings, you can see how you stack up to other players, as well as see what weapons other people use. Find out what weapon works for you! Useful to see how well you do against a particular person. Your ratings change each time you reload!

Boring Man Guides Learn and Teach

With Boring Man Guides, you can read guides that will help your rating, and take you to the next level. Guides are written by the players for the players! They will also cover a wider range of topics, including map building, weapon specific things, and how to earn achievements.

Ladder Tournaments Ready to put your skills to the test?

Ladder tournaments let you strut your stuff in front of a live audience. Showcase what you learned and bring glory to your name! Winning a tournament gives you bragging rights, and real practice against other players.
